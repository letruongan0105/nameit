Welcome to Name it

How can we make an innovator?

Work changes fast. Education and hiring move slower. Youth, migrants, and local communities seek dignity through work yet face a skills mismatch, weak networks, and few entry points. SDG8 calls for decent jobs and inclusive growth. We need innovators who solve real problems and bring income to families and neighborhoods. NameIt grows such innovators through action. Simulation challenges expose real constraints and tradeoffs. Playbooks and templates remove confusion. Mentor and peer reviews bring fast, honest feedback. Each cycle ends with public evidence: shipped work, verified CV bullets on LinkedIn, portfolio links, or a tested venture plan. Signals employers trust. Foundations for self employment. NameIt serves young people, communities, and social tech startups worldwide. The platform joins learning with doing, so skills become work and work creates impact. Join to learn and earn by doing.

Name It is a global initiative equipping young leaders with fintech and sustainability education. We provide young students a toolkit to create sustainable solutions to our world's problems. However, we don't just expose youth to financial technology & sustainable practices: we teach them how to use these skills to build real-world solutions.

Individuals

NameIt supports career planning and skill growth so you can earn online or work in technology firms. The innovator curriculum covers high-value digital skills such as programming, cloud, graphic design, data analytics, AI, and accounting services, plus technical English and workplace skills, plus entrepreneurship and freelancing. You learn by doing through simulations, briefs, and mentored tasks. Each cycle ends with a verified CV bullet, a public project card, or a tested micro-venture aligned with SDG8. Startups

NameIt upskills teams on specialized technologies and strengthens executives through capacity building and strategy support. We co design hiring through challenge based assessments, map global market openings, and connect partner networks across regions. Outcomes include faster onboarding, clearer roadmaps, and first pilots in new markets.

Social Community

NameIt hosts dialogue and hands-on engineering design for sustainable development under SDG8. We run open webinars and focused micro workshops. We keep an active Discord for peer help and mentorship. The aim is a practical network where briefs reach doers and paid work follows.
